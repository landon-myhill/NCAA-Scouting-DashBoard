Production Score (0–100)

Measures raw counting stats, scaled to position-specific caps so guards and bigs are compared fairly.

ComponentWeightCap (Guard)Cap (Forward)Cap (Center)How It Works
Scoring (PPG)50 pts23.023.019.0PPG / Cap, maxed at 1.0, then x50
Playmaking (APG)25 pts8.05.04.0APG / Cap, maxed at 1.0, then x25
Rebounding (RPG)15 pts6.09.012.0RPG / Cap, maxed at 1.0, then x15
Steals (SPG)10 pts2.5 (all positions)SPG / 2.5, maxed at 1.0, then x10
Blocks (BPG)10 pts2.5 (all positions)BPG / 2.5, maxed at 1.0, then x10

Example: A guard with 20 PPG, 6 APG, 4 RPG, 1.5 SPG, 0.5 BPG → (20/23)x50 + (6/8)x25 + (4/6)x15 + (1.5/2.5)x10 + (0.5/2.5)x10 = 43.5 + 18.8 + 10.0 + 6.0 + 2.0 = 80.3

Efficiency Score (0–100+)

Rewards players who produce efficiently. Includes a usage-efficiency bonus for high-volume shot creators.

ComponentWeightHow It Works
PER28 ptsPER / 30, maxed at 1.0, then x28
eFG%12 pts(eFG% – 35) / 25, maxed at 1.0, then x12
TS%8 pts(TS% – 40) / 25, maxed at 1.0, then x8
BPM8 pts(BPM + 5) / 15, maxed at 1.0, then x8
FG%6 pts(FG% – 35) / 25, maxed at 1.0, then x6
3P%22 pts (6 for C)(3P% – 25) / 15, maxed at 1.0. Guards/wings weighted heavily; centers less so
Usage-Efficiency Bonusup to 20 ptsIf USG% > 24 AND TS% > 56: (USG% – 24) / 10 x 20. Rewards high-volume efficient scorers

Final Formula
raw = Production x 0.38 + Efficiency x 0.25 + Impact x 0.15 + Two-Way x 0.10 + bonuses - penalties
final = (raw x class_mult x conf_mult x pos_mult) + height_bonus
